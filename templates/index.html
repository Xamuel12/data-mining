About the Project

CultureExplore is built on the RIYE Cultural Dataset - a comprehensive survey capturing the rich cultural heritage of Ogun State communities. The platform applies K-Means, DBSCAN, and Hierarchical Clustering to group towns with similar cultural profiles.
